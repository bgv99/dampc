### Keywords
Since these keywords are reserved for the language, you can not use them as a name for variables, functions or anything similar.

- `break`
- `const`
- `else`
- `enum`
- `for`
- `function`
- `if`
- `include`
- `macro`
- `next`
- `return`
- `scope`
- `static`
- `struct`
- `to`
- `var`
- `while`
